The spiritual heart
of HaNachal

Spacious and welcoming, HaNachal’s shul serves as both sanctuary and community center; a gathering place for tefillos, simchas, and events

Light, airy, and layered in soft natural tones, the main shul is designed to immediately uplift the hearts and spirits of its mispallilim. It comfortably seats 300.

A secondary shul off to the side provides an intimate setting for early or breakaway manyanim.

Our elegant 250-person simcha hall offers the perfect backdrop for bar mitzvahs, brissim, kiddushim more.

A modern men's mikvah is set on the sanctuary’s ground floor. It features 3 temperature-controlled waters, natural stone tiling, and modern finishes.

A feature-packed rooftop playground, level with the residential spaces, creates a joyful space for your little ones to run and play during shul and all week long.
