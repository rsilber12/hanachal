Come home to peace
and perfection

HaNachal welcomes residents and guests at the end of a private access road, offering a quiet, refined arrival experience.

Each building features an elegant lobby with high-end natural finishes, creating a sense of warmth and sophistication.

With 24-hour security, valet service, and concierge assistance, every arrival is effortless and secure.

Indoors, residential lounges in each building offer a cozy, welcoming space to work, socialize, and relax. Two fully-equipped, state-of-the-art fitness centers support an active lifestyle, rain or shine.

Outdoors, 11 dunams of private parks and playgrounds beckon. With plenty of green space for relaxing, picnicking, biking and soaking in the views of the reserve, time moves slower here.
